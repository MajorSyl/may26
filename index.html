Rotary Club of Freetown-Sunset

Service Above Self — District 9101

Who We Are

The Rotary Club of Freetown-Sunset is a dedicated humanitarian service club based in Freetown, Sierra Leone. Under Rotary District 9101, our diverse network of professionals unites to execute sustainable community projects.

Our Core Pillars of Impact

  • Water & Sanitation: Clean solar-powered borehole well installations.
  • Basic Literacy & Education: Community library refurbishments and textbook distribution.
  • Maternal Clinic Support: Safe birth kits and solar labor lighting for maternal wards.
  • Environmental Protection: Coastal mangrove reforestation campaigns.

Join Our Fellowships

We host weekly physical fellowship dinners every Thursday at 6:30 PM at the Lagoonda Hotel, Cape Road, Aberdeen, Freetown. Visiting Rotarians and local community guests are warmly welcome.
